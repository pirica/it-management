# Modules Overview

## Equipment
Track IT assets and related details, with support for image uploads.

## Printers
Manage printer inventory and supply status.

## Workstations
Track workstation records and assignments.

## Tickets
Create and manage support tickets, including photo attachments.

## Inventory
Track consumables and stock levels.

## Users
Manage system users and access.

## Departments
Organize and assign department-level ownership.

## Employees
Store and manage employee records.

## Companies
Support data partitioning for multiple companies.
